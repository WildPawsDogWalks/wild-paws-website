About Me

Hi, I'm Emily — the person behind Wild Paws. I'm a life-long dog lover and animal behaviour university graduate. I love to keep tails wagging and noses sniffing when you can't be there. I know how busy life can get, that's where I come in. I will pick your dog up, safely transport them to the park (after picking up a couple of other doggie friends on the way), a wonderful Wild Paws walk, and back home safely, ready to relax.

I always have your dog's welfare as my number one priority (fun being a close second) and treat them as though they are my own. Growing up with dogs at home, I know how special they are — truly part of the family!

With me, your dog can make new friends, explore the great outdoors, get lots of love and cuddles, all while you experience a guilt-free day away from your pooch. I make sure to keep the groups small (usually 3–4 dogs) so each dog gets lots of attention, plus I send picture/video updates after every walk so you can share the fun from wherever you are.

My Services

Group Walks

Small-group adventures for sociable dogs who love spending time with their 'furriends'. Kept small so every dog still gets proper time and attention.

Walks go out in small groups with lots of chance to run, sniff, and socialise. I keep groups small on purpose — usually 3–4 dogs — so each one still gets proper attention and I can keep a close eye on how they're getting on. You'll get a photo or video message after every walk so you know exactly what they got up to.

🌿
🐕‍🦺🌲

Solo Walks

One-on-one time for dogs who like their own space and pace. Full attention, no distractions — just your dog and the best parks in South Liverpool.

Your dog gets me all to themselves — no distractions, no other dogs, just a proper one-on-one adventure. This is ideal for nervous or reactive dogs, puppies still finding their feet, or dogs who simply prefer their own company. I'll pick routes that suit them and go at their pace. You get a photo and message after every walk.

🐕
🏠🪴

Drop-In Visits

A check-in visit with loads of fuss when you can't make it home — usually 30 minutes long. Perfect for busy days or dogs who just need a midday pick-me-up.

I come to you — let myself in, have some playtime either indoors or outside, make sure they're fed and settled, then lock up on the way out. Perfect for long work days, unexpected late finishes, or dogs who just need a midday break. I'll send you a message once I'm done so you're never left wondering.
